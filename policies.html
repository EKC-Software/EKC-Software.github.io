Child Safety Standards

At EKC Software, the safety and well-being of our users, especially children, is our highest priority. Our applications are designed to prevent misuse and ensure a secure environment for all users.

  • No in-app messaging is allowed between users.
  • Users cannot acquire other users' email addresses through the app.
  • Users have the option to add a social media tag for another account, but no images or inappropriate content are permitted.
  • There is no allowance, implication, or possibility of child sexual abuse content in any of our applications.

We provide in-app methods for users to report issues quickly and effectively. Additionally, if you need further assistance or wish to report any concerns, you can contact us directly at:

Email: softwareekc@gmail.com

Your safety and trust are important to us, and we are committed to addressing any issues promptly.
